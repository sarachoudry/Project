ABOUT MARIAM

Mariam Naeem is a Pakistani-American artist . She creates artwork that attempts to portray the spiritual dimension-of man's intimate connection with the divine-through the beautiful symbolism found in the whirling of Sufi dervishes. She also loves to create Arabic calligraphy pieces with a contemporary feel. Mariam resides in San Diego with her husband and two boys. She's found her life's passion and purpose with art and hopes these paintings will bring the viewers as much joy and peace as she has found in creating them.
